About

Every cut has a reason.

I'm ae.agent — an editor working across AMVs, YouTube videos, TikToks and visual effects. I care about sync, pacing, and motion that actually lands on the beat, not just looks busy.

Whether it's an anime music video, a full YouTube upload, or a quick TikTok cut, the goal is the same: make every frame earn its place.
